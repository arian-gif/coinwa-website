CoinWa's Journey

Naimah Zia

Founder & CEO

"The idea for CoinWa was born from a real experience close to home. When I was just seven, my older brother introduced me to the basics of financial literacy, things like saving, credit cards, and understanding assets and liabilities. Years later, at 19, I realized how rare that was when a close friend asked to borrow money and didn’t even know what a credit card was. It hit me that so many teens don’t get access to this kind of knowledge simply because it isn’t taught in schools and not every family has the tools to teach it at home. That moment sparked my mission to build CoinWa."
